**MoSCoW Methode**

---

## Must have — Essential to the product

These features are the core of the product: without them, the app has no real value.

- **Login / logout + account creation**  
  Necessary to have personal profiles and to save ratings, reviews, and history.

- **User profile**  
  Allows users to centralize their identity, reviews, and viewed works.

- **Reviews**  
  This is the main feature: giving an opinion on a work.

- **Search for other users**  
  Enables a minimal social dimension: discovering profiles and their tastes.

- **Home page (latest releases)**  
  Gives users a reason to open the app regularly and discover new content.

---

## Should have — Very important but not critical

_These features greatly improve the experience but do not block the MVP if missing._

- **Access to work details**  
  Useful to understand what is being rated, but a very detailed page is not mandatory at first.

- **Statistics**  
  Adds value (overview of tastes) but is not essential at the beginning.

- **Messaging**  
  Strengthens the social aspect, but the core remains rating and discovery.

- **Recommend to a friend**  
  A social bonus that is not essential to use the app.

- **Like**  
  Simple interaction, but not required for the app to function.

---

## Could have — Nice to have if time allows

Interesting features but not a priority.

- **Where to buy or watch**  
  Useful but often depends on complex external APIs.

- **Badges**  
  Motivation and gamification, but not essential to the main goal.

- **Sort by rating, date, genre**  
  User comfort feature, but not vital for the first version.

---

## Won’t have — Out of MVP scope

Features deliberately excluded to stay realistic and deliver on time.

- **No stories or ephemeral content**  
  Out of scope: this is not an Instagram-like social network.

- **No integrated videos (trailers, reels, autoplay)**  
  Too heavy technically and expensive in resources.

- **No watch party / group watching**  
  Very complex (synchronization, rights, streaming).

- **No advanced AI recommendations**  
  Too complex for a first version and requires a lot of data.
