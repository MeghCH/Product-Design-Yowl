## /docs/02_personas 

This document presents four personas representing different user profiles and needs related to culture, entertainment, and recommendation tools.

**Sophia (16, student, reader)**
Represents young readers who want to track books they have read and plan future readings.
Key needs: memory of past readings, simple tracking, reading history visualization.

**Ellie (19, literature student, gamer)**
Represents users who want to connect literature and video games.
Key needs: game recommendations based on literary tastes, narrative-focused discovery, cross-media experiences.

**Benjamin (28, web developer, gamer)**
Represents active gamers who want to optimize their time.
Key needs: knowing game length in advance, personalized recommendations based on time and taste, better game library management.

**Eliot (40, teacher, movie lover)**
Represents film and series enthusiasts who want to remember and organize what they have watched.
Key needs: viewing history, summaries of movies and episodes, recommendations based on directors and genres.

Together, these personas highlight shared themes:
memory of past experiences, personalized recommendations, and better organization of cultural content (books, games, films, series).

**_They serve as a reference to guide design choices and ensure the product responds to real user needs._**
