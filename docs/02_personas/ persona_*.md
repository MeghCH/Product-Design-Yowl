# Persona - Sophia

**Profile**

**Name:** Sophia  
**Age:** 16  
**Occupation:** High school student  
**Interests:** Reading, contemporary literature, sharing books online

**Description**

Sophia loves reading. After finishing a book, she usually sells it or gives it away, so she never keeps them. Over time, she has realized that she can't remember all the books she has read.  
She would like to find an app that allows her to **keep track of all the books she has already read**, as well as **those she wants to read in the future**, because to her knowledge, **no app really meets this need** for readers.

**Goals**

- Easily keep track of the books she has read.
- Keep track of books she wants to read later.
- Be able to view her reading history.

**Motivations**

- Organize her passion for reading.
- Find forgotten titles.
- Feel a sense of progress in her literary culture.

**Behaviors**

- Reads daily, mainly on paper.
- Resells or gives away her books after reading them.
- Already uses mobile apps, but not a platform dedicated to reading.

**Pain Points**

- Forgets the titles and authors of the books she has read.
- Can't find a simple app that suits her needs.
- Frustration at not being able to revisit her past readings.

# Persona - Ellie

**Profile**

**Name:** Ellie  
**Age:** 19  
**Occupation:** Modern literature student  
**Interests:** Reading, video games, fantasy

**Description**

Ellie reads voraciously and recently discovered that some of the books she has read have been adapted into video games. This discovery led her to explore **the world of gaming**.  
After completing her first game, she realized she would like to start a new one, but **didn't know which one to choose**.  
She would therefore like to have **a tool that can recommend video games that match her literary tastes and the narrative genres she enjoys.**

**Goals**

- Discover new games inspired by her reading tastes.
- Find games with a universe or narrative similar to her favorite books.
- Centralize her reading and gaming experiences.

**Motivations**

- Explore new forms of storytelling.
- Connect her passions: literature and video games.
- Save time searching for new games.

**Behaviors**

- Plays mainly on PC and console.
- Follows literary and gaming influencers on social media.
- Often seeks personalized recommendations.

**Pain Points**

- Difficulty finding games that match his literary tastes.
- Few apps combine reading and video games.
- Feels like he wastes time searching for new games to try.

# Persona - Benjamin

**Profile**

**Name:** Benjamin  
**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Web developer  
**Interests:** Video games, technology, Steam platforms

**Description**

Benjamin is a big fan of video games. He plays regularly, both to relax and to challenge himself.  
However, he is tired of **never knowing how long it will take him to finish a game**, because on Steam, the platform he uses the most, this information is not clearly indicated.  
He would also like to **receive recommendations for games tailored to his personal tastes** and available time, in order to optimize his gaming experience.

**Goals**

- Know the average length of a game before starting it.
- Receive personalized game recommendations based on his tastes and schedule.
- Effectively manage his game library.

**Motivations**

- Optimize his leisure time.
- Discover new games without wasting time searching.
- Have a more planned gaming experience that fits his lifestyle.

**Behaviors**

- Plays mainly on PC via Steam.
- Often checks reviews and ratings before buying a game.
- Prefers narrative or immersive games, but sometimes lacks the time to finish them.

**Pain Points**

- Doesn't know how long a game will take to complete before starting it.
- Frustration at not being able to anticipate the time needed to finish a game.
- Lack of relevant recommendations based on their playing style and preferences.

# Persona - Eliot

**Profile**

**Name:** Eliot  
**Age:** 40  
**Occupation:** Teacher and passionate film buff  
**Interests:** Movies, TV series, streaming platforms

**Description**

Eliot is a film buff who is starting to take an interest in TV series.  
Over time, he has realized that he has seen a lot of films, but **doesn't always remember them clearly**: he sometimes remembers the title, but not the story.  
He would like to have an app that allows him to **list all the movies he has seen**, **find summaries**, and **receive recommendations for TV shows and movies** based on his cinematic tastes or **favorite directors**.  
In addition, he would also like to **be able to view summaries of the episodes of series he has watched**, so that he can more easily remember their content.

**Goals**

- Retrieve and organize his movie memories.
- Discover new series related to his tastes.
- Easily access reliable summaries of movies and episodes he has seen.

**Motivations**

- No longer forget the movies and series he has watched.
- Cultivate his passion for cinema and directors.
- Simplify the tracking of his viewing activity.

**Behaviors**

- Regularly watches movies and TV shows on multiple platforms (Netflix, Prime, etc.).
- Often checks reviews and ratings before watching a movie.
- Occasionally shares recommendations with friends.

**Pain Points**

- Forgets the plots of movies or episodes they have already seen.
- No app centralizes their viewing history and preferences.
- Difficulty finding relevant recommendations based on their specific tastes.
