## Description

### Prototype – Desktop Version

- We start on the home page when we are not logged in.  
  We click on the yellow **“Log in”** button at the top right.
- We arrive on the login page: this is the page where we can create an account, reset our password, or simply log in.  
  We click on **“Log in.”**
- We arrive on our profile page.  
  On this page, we click on the **“Books”** button in the filters.
- We arrive on the page with all the books (“read” and “to read”).  
  We click on the second book.
- We arrive on the information page of this book.  
  We click on the **“Reviews”** button in the filters.
- We arrive on the “Reviews” section of the selected book.  
  Then we click on **“Research.”**
- We arrive on another user’s profile page.  
  We click on the **“Home”** button in the header.
- We go back to the main page of the site, where we click on the **“Profile”** button in the header.
- We return to our profile page.  
  We click on the red **“Log out”** button.
- We return to the login page.

### Prototype – Mobile Version

- We start on the home page when we are not logged in.  
  We click on the yellow **“Log in”** button at the bottom right.
- We arrive on the login page: this is the page where we can create an account, reset our password, or simply log in.  
  We click on **“Log in.”**
- We arrive on our profile page.  
  On this page, we click on the **“Books”** button in the filters.
- We arrive on the page with all the books (“read” and “to read”), where we can scroll.  
  We click on the second book.
- We arrive on the information page of this book.  
  We scroll, then in the filters we scroll to the right and click on the **“Reviews”** button.
- We arrive on the “Reviews” section of the selected book.  
  Then we click on the icon at the top left to go back.
- We return to the book information page, then we click again on the icon to go back.
- We go back to our profile page.  
  We click on the **“Home”** button in the navbar.
- We return to the main page, then we click on the **“Profile”** button in the navbar.
- We return to our profile page, then we click on the red **“Log out”** button.
- We end on the login page.
