# Media Catalog App
### Centralize, track and share cultural works

Books · Video Games · Movies · TV Series

---

## Problem

Cultural works are currently spread across multiple platforms.

- Movies / TV series: Letterboxd, IMDb
- TV series: Serializd
- Video games: Backloggd, Steam
- Books: Goodreads

--> There is no simple way to have a global view of one’s cultural consumption.

---

## Target Users

### Regular cultural consumers
- Watch movies and TV series
- Play video games
- Read books
- Want to track what they have seen or plan to see

### Opinion-driven users
- Enjoy rating and writing reviews
- Discover content through other users’ opinions

### Students and young professionals
- Already use cultural tracking apps
- Look for a simple, unified solution

---

## Concept

An application that allows users to:

- Search for cultural works
- Mark them as **To Watch / Seen**
- Give a **rating**
- Write a **review**
- View other users’ profiles and reviews

--> A simple and unified personal and social catalog.

---

## UX Walkthrough

### 1. Authentication
- Sign up / log in

### 2. Search
- Search by title
- Filter by type (book, video game, movie, TV series)

### 3. Work Details
- Main information
- Average rating
- Actions: To Watch / Seen, rating, review

---

## UX Walkthrough (continued)

### 4. Personal Library
- List of the user’s works
- Filter by status

### 5. User Profile
- Public profile
- Ratings and reviews visible to others

---

## MVP Scope

### Included
- User authentication
- Work search
- Work detail page
- Status: To Watch / Seen
- Ratings
- Short reviews
- Personal library
- Public user profiles

---

## Out of MVP Scope

- Follow system, feed, notifications
- Comments on reviews
- Personalized recommendations
- Advanced tracking (episodes, pages, playtime)
- Moderation and monetization

---

## Conclusion

- Clear problem
- Simple solution
- Realistic MVP
- Strong foundation for future evolution

An application to better consume, remember, and share culture.
