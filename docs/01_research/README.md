## /docs/01_research - README

This folder contains the deliverables from the user and market research phase.

**benchmark.md**
Analysis of existing or similar solutions.
Goal: identify best practices, limitations, and potential differentiation points for our product.

**interviews.md**
Notes from at least 3 structured interviews with classmates.
Content highlights their needs, frustrations, habits, and expectations, based on observed facts.

**problem_statement.md**
Clear and concise formulation of the problem the product aims to solve, based on insights from the benchmark and interviews.

**objectives_constraints.md**
List of the project’s measurable objectives, as well as identified assumptions, risks, and constraints.

**_All these documents provide a factual foundation to guide product design and development decisions._**
